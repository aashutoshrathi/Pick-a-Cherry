Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how "Pick-a-Cherry" collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection and Usage

The "Pick-a-Cherry" extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data, browsing history, or any other user-related information.

Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions to function correctly:

  • Access to GitHub pages: This is necessary to insert a cherry icon next to commit hashes.
  • Clipboard Access: Used to copy the "git cherry-pick $commit-sha" command when the cherry icon is clicked. No data is stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Security

Since "Pick-a-Cherry" does not store or transmit user data, there are no security risks related to personal data exposure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
